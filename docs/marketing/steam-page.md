# AstroCode — Steam Store Page

## Short Description

Stranded on an alien planet with nothing but a Python terminal. Write real code to program robots, wire circuits to power your base, and train AI to survive. Your code is your lifeline.

## About This Game

You crash-land alone on Thyra-7 — a planet that killed three expeditions before you. Your ship is wrecked, your oxygen is dropping, and the only tool that still works is a code terminal. Write a function to fix the O2 recycler or suffocate. Welcome to day one.

AstroCode is an automation survival game where every problem is solved with real code and real circuits. Program robots in a Python-like language to gather resources, build sensor networks, and automate your base. Wire breadboard circuits to power your machines, shield them from electromagnetic storms, and connect sensors that keep you alive. As your colony grows, your code evolves — from simple scripts to event-driven systems to neural networks that let your robots think for themselves.

Eight alien biomes stretch from salt flats to frozen methane lakes to underground lava cathedrals — each with unique resources, deadly hazards, and engineering puzzles that demand new skills. Meet survivors and AI remnants from failed expeditions who teach you circuits, physics, and machine learning through hands-on challenges. Piece together why every prior crew went silent, and discover that the planet isn't your enemy — it's a system you need to understand.

The game gets easier as you get smarter. Early days are desperate manual survival. Late game is watching your robot army execute code you wrote while you explore the unknown. Every line you write, every circuit you build, every model you train — it all compounds. By day 60, you're not just surviving. You're engineering a rescue.

## Features

- Write real Python-like code in an in-game terminal with syntax highlighting, error messages, and a step-through debugger
- Program 8 robot types from inert hardware into autonomous workers — gatherers, scouts, builders, farmers, and swarm drones
- Build circuits on a visual breadboard — resistors, capacitors, microcontrollers, sensors, and motors with real failure states
- Survive 60 in-game days across 8 handcrafted biomes, from bioluminescent marshes to obsidian mountains crackling with electromagnetic arcs
- Progress from `print("hello")` to training neural networks in one continuous survival campaign
- Automate everything — resource gathering, base defense, crop farming, exploration — your code replaces the grind
- Uncover the mystery of three lost expeditions through crew journals, broken drones, and an alien intelligence woven into the planet itself
- Design sensor networks, predict storms with regression models, and classify alien specimens with decision trees
- Meet four unique NPCs — a circuit-obsessed survivor, a glitching AI drone, an ancient alien organism, and a holographic research mentor
- Export your code and circuit designs as real files — skills you build in-game transfer to the real world

## Early Access

### Why Early Access?

AstroCode combines programming, circuit design, and machine learning into a single survival experience. Getting the difficulty curve right — making it challenging enough for experienced coders while accessible to complete beginners — requires thousands of players testing and feeding back. Early Access lets us tune puzzles, expand the coding API, and add community-requested robot behaviors based on how real players approach problems.

### How long will Early Access last?

Approximately 12 months. We plan to launch Early Access with the first 30 in-game days (4 biomes, 3 NPCs, full programming and circuit systems). The remaining 30 days, 4 biomes, ML tier, and endgame will be added in free updates during the Early Access period.

### What is the current state of the Early Access version?

The core gameplay loop is complete: coding terminal, circuit builder, robot programming, base building, day/night cycle, and the first four biomes with full quest lines. Save/load, keybinding customization, and accessibility options are functional.

### Will the price change after Early Access?

The Early Access price is $16.99. The full release price will be $19.99.
